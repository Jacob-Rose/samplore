What is Samplore?

You know that new sound you're looking for... well listen to this.

Samplore is a complementary program that works with the DAW you already use. It features near-latency-free sample previewing from any point in the sample. Try cutting off the start of a sample and see if it could work, try listening to it backward. Samplore is made to be a playground to inspire creativity.

Organize your samples over time, save sounds for later if they don't fit the current song, and never lose track of great samples again.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a subscription-based program?

No! Samplore is free and open source. It's not made to sell you samples - it's made to help you explore and organize the samples you already own.

Does this work with my DAW?

Yes! Samplore works alongside any DAW through simple drag-and-drop functionality. It's been tested with Ableton Live, FL Studio, Logic Pro, and more.

How will this help me?

Samplore helps you not just find the sounds you're looking for, but discover new sounds you didn't know existed in your library. With lightning-fast previewing and smart organization, finding inspiration is just a few clicks away.

Is it really open source?

Yes! The entire codebase is available on GitHub. You can contribute, fork it, or build it yourself.
